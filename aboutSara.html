More About Me

What are your favorite hobbies?

My favorite hobbies are playing the piano from time to time, painting watercolors, and watching nature and crime documentaries. I also enjoy spending time with my friends, going out to eat at restaurants or partying together. In relation to my career I love visiting architectural landmarks and studying their design principles.

What's your dream job?

My ideal job is to have my own architectural firm where I aspire to lead a team of designers and collaborate with experts from various fields to create inspiring spaces that enhance quality of life and promote social well-being.

What is for you your future city?

For me, the future city is one that prioritizes sustainability, resilience, and inclusivity. It's a city where green spaces and pedestrian-friendly environments are seamlessly integrated into the urban fabric, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. Advanced technologies such as renewable energy systems, smart infrastructure, and digital connectivity play a crucial role in optimizing resource usage and enhancing efficiency.

The future city fosters a sense of community and belonging through mixed-use developments, vibrant public spaces, and diverse cultural amenities that celebrate local identity and heritage. It's a city that embraces adaptable and flexible design strategies to accommodate changing needs and evolving trends, ensuring longevity and relevance in an ever-changing world.
