Our vision of the future cities in 2050

If we combine all our ideas during the conversations, our joint mood board is the one above. We opt for a green and futuristic city. The greenery is important because nature is more important today than ever, something we must not destroy. We love futurism with some sports facilities and tall buildings, as well as flying objects. Additionally, in the bottom left of the mood board, there is an upgraded train because trains in all countries look so ugly now, and we strongly believe in the future of public transportation.
